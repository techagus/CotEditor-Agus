Define completion candidates in CotEditor

You can create your own completion list by customizing the corresponding syntax. After choosing a syntax and clicking Edit (see Define a syntax), select Completion List from the menu.

FieldDescription
CompletionsThe words you put here appear in the completion list.

If no words here, the words generated from your syntax coloring definition will appear in the list.
